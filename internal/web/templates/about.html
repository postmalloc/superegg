About superegg

superegg is a personal news desk. It pulls from the feeds and sites you care about, extracts the article, summarizes it, and keeps everything in one quiet place.

The point is not to turn reading into a game. It is to help you scan quickly, open what matters, and keep some memory of what you have already seen.

It is meant to feel more like a tidy workbench than a social feed: dense, simple, and out of the way.
